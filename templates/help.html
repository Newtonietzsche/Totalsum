Help for Using the Text Summarization Interface

1. Upload the PDF

To start, please upload the PDF file you want to summarize:

  1. Click the "Choose PDF File" button in the "Upload PDF" section.
  2. Select the PDF file from your computer.
  3. The file will be uploaded to the form.

2. Summary Settings

Adjust the settings according to your needs:

  1. Type of summarization:
    • General sum: For a general summary of the text.
    • Method-oriented sum: For a summary focused on the method.
    • Goal-oriented sum: For a summary focused on the goal of the article. Adapted to know what this article stand for.
  2. Language of summarization:
    • English: Make the summary in English.
    • French: Make the summary in French.
    • German: Make the summary in German.
  3. OpenAI Key: Enter your OpenAI key to activate the summarization service. This application is working with the openAI

3. Launch the Summary

After configuring the settings:

  1. Click the "Launch summary" button. It can take few minutes to process.
  2. The summarized text will appear in the "Text preview" text area.

4. Download the Results

You can download the summary in different formats:

  1. Podcast: Click the "Download MP3" button to download the audio summary.
  2. PDF: Click the "Download PDF" button to download the summary in PDF format.
